About

Welcome to my virtual humble abode!

As previously stated, my name is Courtney Chu and I am a Computer Engineering (CPE) Major at the California Polytechnic State University San Luis Obispo (Cal Poly SLO).

I grew up in a small town just north of Los Angeles called South Pasadena. It is best known for being a part of Route 66, the Fair Oaks' Pharmacy and Soda Fountain, and for originally being an ostrich farm. There is a large on-going dispute about whether South Pasadena should be divided up so that the 710 freeway can continue unhindered. My hometown fought against the continuation of the 710 freeway from the 1970's and only recently has been reassured of their success.

My introduction to programming was an AP Computer Science class I took during my final year of high school. As beginner programmers in Java, y classmates and I were taught how to print to the console, create classes and subclasses, and eventually how to create a mini game based on GridWorld in BlueJ.
